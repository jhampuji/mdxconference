Main Venue

Middlesex University Mauritius

Coastal Road, 90203, Uniciti, Flic-en-Flac

Located in the lively and scenic touristic haven of Flic en Flac is pleased to invite you to the Symposium on Equality, Dignity and Wellbeing: LGBTQIA+ Perspectives. Flic en Flac a sublime location along the west coast of Mauritius is one of the longest beaches on the island. The Flic en Flac beach is a great spot for embarking on a diving or snorkeling trip to explore the blue lagoon and coral reef.
