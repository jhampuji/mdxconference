The Symposium

This symposium being organised in collaboration with Young Queer Alliance (YQA) under the project "Sustaining Development of LGBTQIA+ Leaders in Mauritius", funded by The Other Foundation.

The symposium will consist of panel discussions, presentation of latest LGBTQIA+ research and initiatives in the region. Local and regional scholars from South-East African countries as well as community leaders are invited to submit their research abstracts or summary reports for community-driven outputs. Regional scholars selected to present their research at the conference will have to make their own flight arrangements. Accommodation and transport arrangement to the symposium will be covered for regional scholars coming from outside of Mauritius.

Why Attend?

  • Attendance is free for all
  • Community Engagement:
    • Raise awareness and bridge information gaps concerning LGBTQIA+ experiences
  • Knowledge Exchange:
    • Learn about recent academic research and community-driven outputs on LGBQIA+ data
  • Networking Opportunity:
    • Connect with scholars, professionals, and community champions
    • Engage with regional and local stakeholders engaged in defending Human Rights for all
